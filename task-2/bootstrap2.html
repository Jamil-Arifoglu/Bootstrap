Bayraktar TB2

The Bayraktar TB2 is a Tactical Armed / UAV System, developed and manufactured by Baykar. A highly sophisticated design that provides all solutions that operator may need in one integrated system. The system consists of Bayraktar TB2 Armed / UAV Platform, Ground Control Station, Ground Data Terminal, Remote Display Terminal, Advanced Base with Generator and Trailer modules. Thanks to Baykar's technological accumulation and capabilities, the entire system is produced indigenously.

Özdemir Bayraktar

Pioneer of locally-produced UCAVs, entrepreneur, certified engineer and industrialist Özdemir Bayraktar... He devoted the greater part of his existence over the last 20 years of his life in front of a computer or a bench, living in his workplace to achieve absolute independence for our country... Özdemir Bayraktar was born in the village of Garipçe in Istanbul's Sarıyer district in 1949 to his father, Fisherman Lütfi Reis, who was originally from the district of Sürmene in Trabzon Province. Bayraktar graduated from Kabataş High School. He continued on to higher education at Istanbul Technical University in 1967 in the Mechanical Engineering Department and went on to graduate with high marks in 1972. After his graduation, he worked as a research assistant to ITU Motors' Chair, Professor İsmail Hakkı Öz, for 2 years and completed his Master's degree with a focus on internal combustion engines.

Bayraktar
TB2

Bayraktar TB2 Tactical S/UAV System, developed and put into use by Baykar, is an integrated network-based system architecture that offers all solutions to the user. The system consists of Bayraktar TB2 S/UAV Platform, Ground Control Station, Ground Data Terminal, Remote Display Terminal, Forward Base and Generator and Trailer modules. Thanks to Baykar's technological capabilities and capabilities, the entire system is produced locally and nationally. IN THE SKY FOR 500 THOUSAND HOURS Bayraktar TB2 Tactical Armed Unmanned Aerial Vehicle is an unmanned aerial vehicle in the medium altitude-long stay-in-air class (MALE) for reconnaissance and intelligence missions. It has fully autonomous taxi, takeoff, landing and normal navigation capabilities with its triple redundant avionics systems and sensor fusion architecture. The TB2, which has flown more than 500,000 hours, has been actively serving since 2014 within the Turkish Armed Forces, Gendarmerie and Police Department. Currently, hundreds of Bayraktar TB2 S/UAV Platforms are working in the inventory of Turkey, Qatar, Ukraine, Azerbaijan and delivery countries. Bayraktar TB2 broke the record for airtime (27 Hours and 3 Minutes) and altitude (25,30 feet) in Turkish aviation history. Bayraktar TB2 is also the first aircraft exported in this scale.
